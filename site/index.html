A real exchange underneath.

The cartoon racing game is the cockpit. Every ride drives the same reconciler and accounting that runs real money — bounded, cross-checked, and self-custodied.

Thumb on a throttle

Press and your position grows; let go and it eases off. The throttle is your size — leverage you can feel.

~30 ms on an ER

Gasless, session-signed ticks on a MagicBlock Ephemeral Rollup, marked against an in-rollup Pyth Lazer oracle.

Real, self-custodied

A bounded reconciler mirrors your ride into real Flash Trade positions — keys never leave your phone.

Keyless guardian

An off-phone watcher holds zero keys. It can only fire your own pre-signed flatten if the loss floor is hit.

Hard bounds, always

Fuel × leverage, an independent Pyth cross-check, a per-action cap, and a floor-derived stop on every ride.

The chain can't trade you

Rollup state is advisory only. Real money stays bounded and self-custodied — the chain can never directly authorize a trade.
